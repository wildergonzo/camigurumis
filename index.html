Meet the Artist

Carminia Baldelomar

Born and raised in Bolivia, I discovered my passion for the arts at an early age. After studying Arts in Bolivia, I spent years expressing my creativity through painting, capturing the vibrant colors and rich culture of my homeland on canvas.

My artistic journey took a delightful turn when I discovered the wonderful world of amigurumi. What started as a personal hobby quickly blossomed into a deep love for creating these adorable crocheted companions. Each piece I create carries the same attention to detail and artistic vision that I brought to my paintings.

Today, I'm thrilled to share my handcrafted amigurumi creations with people around the world. Every stitch is made with love, bringing joy and comfort to those who welcome these little characters into their homes. From cute animals to magical creatures, each amigurumi tells its own special story.
